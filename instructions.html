How the Game Works When the game starts, a ball will begin moving around the screen. At the bottom of the screen there is a paddle controlled by the player. The player must move the paddle left or right using the arrow keys to keep the ball in play. If the ball hits the paddle, it bounces back upward. At the top of the screen there are rows of bricks. When the ball hits a brick, the brick breaks and disappears. Each brick destroyed adds points to the player's score.
